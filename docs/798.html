798 - What is the importance of soil health in farming?

Soil health is very important in farming for several reasons:

  1. Grows Stronger Plants: Healthy soil has the right amount of water, air, and nutrients for plants to grow strong and healthy.
  2. Stores Water: Good soil helps store water, so plants can use it when they need it, which means less irrigation is needed.
  3. Keeps Pests Away: Healthy soil has good bacteria and other tiny organisms that help keep pests and diseases away from plants.
  4. Makes Food Taste Better: Soil health can affect the taste and quality of fruits and vegetables.
  5. Helps the Environment: Healthy soil also helps clean the water and air around us.

Overall, taking care of soil health is important for growing healthy food, protecting the environment, and keeping farmers' crops strong and healthy.
