1390 - How do we save energy in daily life.

There are many simple ways to save energy in our daily life. Here are a few:

  1. Turn off lights and electronics: When not in use, switch off lights, TVs, and computers to save energy.
  2. Use energy-efficient bulbs: LED bulbs use less energy than regular bulbs.
  3. Take shorter showers: Use less hot water to save energy and water.
  4. Walk or bike: Instead of using cars or bikes with motors, walk or ride a bike for short trips.
  5. Use power strips: Plug electronics like TVs and computers into power strips and turn off the strip when not in use.
  6. Turn off the fridge when away: If you're going out for a long time, turn off the fridge to save energy.
  7. Use natural light: Open curtains and blinds to let sunlight in during the day.
  8. Don't waste energy on standby: Unplug phones, toys, and other devices when charged.

Remember, every small action helps save energy and protects the environment.
