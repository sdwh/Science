769 - How do you properly bait a hook?

To properly bait a hook, follow these steps:

  1. Choose the right bait: Select the bait you want to use, such as a worm, minnow, or lure.
  2. Thread the bait gently: If using a worm or minnow, thread the hook through its body, just under the skin. Try not to hurt the bait.
  3. Cover the hook point: Make sure the hook point is completely covered by the bait.
  4. Don't over-bait: Use just enough bait to cover the hook. Too much bait can make the hook look suspicious to fish.
  5. Secure the bait: For bigger baits like minnows, you can use a bobber stop or a small rubber band to hold the bait on the hook.

Remember, be gentle with the bait and handle the hook carefully to avoid getting hurt.
