1450 - What are the applications of gene transfer technology?

Gene transfer technology is used to put a copy of a gene from one living thing into another. Some of its cool applications are:

  1. Medicine: To make medicines, like insulin for diabetes, cheaper and more available.
  2. Crop improvement: To create crops that can resist pests, need less water, or are more nutritious.
  3. Gene therapy: To treat genetic diseases in humans by replacing a faulty gene with a healthy one.
  4. Vaccines: To produce vaccines that can protect us from diseases.
  5. Pest control: To create animals that can fight pests and reduce pesticide use.
  6. Forensic science: To help identify people or animals, and solve crimes.
  7. Animal breeding: To create animals with desired traits, like better milk production or meat quality.

These are just a few examples, but the possibilities are endless!
