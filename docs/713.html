713 - What are the benefits of walking and using bicycles for short distances?

Walking and using bicycles for short distances have many benefits. Here are some of them:

  1. Stay Healthy: Walking and biking help keep your body active and healthy. They can even help you lose weight or keep your weight under control.

  2. Good for the Environment: Cars can pollute the air, but walking and biking don't make any pollution. This helps keep the air clean.

  3. Save Money: You don't need to buy gas or pay for parking when you walk or bike. This saves your family money.

  4. More Fun: Walking and biking are fun ways to get around. You can see more things and enjoy the outside while you're moving.

  5. Less Traffic: Fewer cars on the road means less traffic. This helps everyone get where they need to go faster.

  6. Better for Your Mind: Being outside and moving can help you feel happy and relaxed.
