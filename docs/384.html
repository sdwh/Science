384 - What are the benefits of preserving natural habitats?

Preserving natural habitats has many benefits. Here are some of them:

  1. Protects animals and plants: Preserving habitats helps animals and plants live safely in their natural environment.
  2. Maintains the food chain: Habitats are connected, and preserving them ensures that animals have food to eat and a home.
  3. Gives us clean air and water: Vegetation in habitats helps clean the air and water for us to breathe and drink.
  4. Prevents extinction: Preserving habitats helps prevent animals and plants from becoming extinct.
  5. Keeps us healthy: Natural habitats have many plants that help us make medicines to cure diseases.

By preserving natural habitats, we help maintain the balance of nature and keep our planet healthy.
