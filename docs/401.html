What are the main types of marine ecosystems?

There are several main types of marine ecosystems:

  1. Coral Reefs: Colorful ecosystems with coral and lots of fish.
  2. Open Ocean: The deep, wide ocean where many animals like whales and dolphins live.
  3. Estuaries: Where rivers meet the ocean, mixing fresh and saltwater, and creating a home for many animals.
  4. Kelp Forests: Taller seaweed forests that provide shelter for sea creatures like seals and sea otters.
  5. Polar Ecosystems: Cold, icy areas at the North and South poles, where animals like penguins and polar bears live.

These are the main types of marine ecosystems, and each one is special in its own way.
