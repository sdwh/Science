787 - How do you start a jogging routine if you're a beginner?

Starting a jogging routine can be fun and easy. Here's a simple step-by-step guide:

  1. Get ready: Wear comfortable shoes and clothes, and dress according to the weather.
  2. Start walking: Begin with short walks (15-20 minutes) to get your body used to movement.
  3. Add jogging: After a few days, try jogging for 1-2 minutes, then walk for 2-3 minutes. Repeat this pattern for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Gradually increase: Each week, add 1-2 minutes to your jogging time and decrease your walking time.
  5. Listen to your body: Rest when you need to, and don't push yourself too hard.
  6. Make it fun: Find a friend or family member to jog with, or listen to music that motivates you.
  7. Stay hydrated: Drink water before, during, and after your jog to stay refreshed.

Remember, starting slow and being consistent is the key to a successful jogging routine.
