629 - What are some safety tips for using electricity at home?

Here are some essential safety tips for using electricity at home:

  1. Don't touch electrical outlets: Avoid touching electrical outlets with your fingers, and don't stick objects like pens or toys into the holes.
  2. Unplug when not in use: Unplug appliances and devices when they're not in use to avoid overheating and fires.
  3. Keep water away: Don't use electrical appliances near water, like in the bathroom or kitchen.
  4. Use proper cords: Use the right cord for each appliance, and avoid overloading outlets with too many cords.
  5. Be careful with wires: Don't touch or play with wires, and keep them out of reach of pets.
  6. Turn off the power: If you need to fix or clean an appliance, turn off the power at the electrical panel first.
  7. Ask an adult for help: If you're not sure about something electrical, ask a grown-up for help.

Remember, safety first!
