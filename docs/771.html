771 - How can you tell if you have a fish on your line?

To know if you have a fish on your line, look out for these signs:

  1. Tug or Pull: If you feel a strong tug or pull on the line, it might be a fish.
  2. Line Movement: If the line starts moving or wiggling, it could be a fish biting.
  3. Bobber Down: If you're using a bobber (a floating device), and it goes under water, it might mean a fish is biting.
  4. Resistance: When you reel in the line, if you feel resistance or hardness, it could be a fish on the other end.

Remember to stay calm, be gentle, and have fun!
