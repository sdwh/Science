1394 - Why must the development of human society be based on the protection of the Earth's natural environment?

The Earth is our home, and its natural environment gives us everything we need to live: air, water, food, and shelter. If we harm the environment, we're harming ourselves. Protecting the Earth's natural environment is crucial because:

  1. It provides us with life's necessities.
  2. It regulates the climate and weather.
  3. It supports an incredible variety of plants and animals.
  4. It helps keep us healthy and happy.

Just like how we clean and take care of our homes, we need to protect the Earth's environment so that future generations can live safely and happily too.
