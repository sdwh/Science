How does weather affect our daily activities?

Weather can affect our daily activities in many ways.

  1. Transportation: Rain or snow might make roads slippery, making it difficult to drive or ride a bike to school.
  2. Outdoor activities: Nice weather is great for playing sports, picnics, or going to the park. But bad weather like heavy rain or extreme heat can ruin our outdoor plans.
  3. Clothing: We wear different clothes depending on the weather. For example, we wear warm clothes in cold weather and light clothes in hot weather.
  4. School and work: Severe weather conditions like hurricanes or blizzards might even close schools or offices for the day.
  5. Air quality: Some weather conditions like smog or smoke can make the air unhealthy to breathe, making it hard for people with respiratory problems.

The weather can either make or break our day, so it's essential to check the forecast before making plans.
