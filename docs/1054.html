1054 - What is the food chain?

A food chain is when animals eat other animals or plants, and it shows how they're connected.

It usually starts with a plant (like grass or a tree), then an animal eats the plant (like a rabbit), then another animal eats that animal (like a hawk), and so on.

For example: Grass (plant) → Rabbit (eats grass) → Hawk (eats rabbit)

This helps show how animals and plants depend on each other for food and survival.
