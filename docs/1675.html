1675 - What is the term for the lowest frequency sound wave that forms a standing wave and the term for the higher frequency sound waves.

The lowest frequency sound wave that forms a standing wave is called the 'fundamental frequency' or 'first harmonic'.

The higher frequency sound waves are called 'overtones' or 'harmonics'. They have higher frequencies than the fundamental frequency and are also part of the standing wave pattern.
