780 - What are some fun camping activities or games to enjoy with friends?

Camping with friends is super fun. Here are some awesome activities you can do:

  1. Nature Scavenger Hunt: Create a list of items to find in nature, like leaves, rocks, or birds. See who can find everything on the list first.
  2. Flashlight Tag: Play tag in the dark using flashlights. It's like regular tag, but way more exciting.
  3. Glow-in-the-Dark Games: Bring glow sticks or glow-in-the-dark balls to play games like capture the flag or soccer.
  4. Campfire Stories: Take turns telling spooky or funny stories around the campfire. You can also make up your own stories together.
  5. Campground Olympics: Set up a mini Olympics with events like a hike, a water balloon toss, or a marshmallow-eating contest.
  6. Stargazing: Spend some time gazing at the stars and trying to find constellations. It's super cool.
  7. Nature Crafts: Collect items from nature, like sticks and rocks, to make crafts like a bird feeder or a dreamcatcher.
  8. Flashlight Hike: Take a hike at night using flashlights to see the trail and the stars.
  9. Group Campfire Cooking: Make a meal together over the campfire, like hot dogs or s'mores.
  10. Ghostly Sounds: Make spooky sounds in the woods using flashlights and other objects. See who can come up with the most creative sounds.
