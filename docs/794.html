What are some tips for jogging in different weather conditions?

When jogging in different weather conditions, here are some tips to stay safe:

Hot Weather: 1. Wear light and breathable clothes. 2. Bring water to stay hydrated. 3. Run early in the morning or late in the evening when it's cooler.

Rainy Weather: 1. Wear water-repellent clothes and shoes. 2. Run on flat surfaces to avoid slipping. 3. Use reflective gear or bright clothes to be seen by cars.

Cold Weather: 1. Wear warm and layered clothes. 2. Cover your head, ears, and hands. 3. Wear proper shoes with traction to prevent slipping.

Dark Weather: 1. Wear reflective or bright clothes. 2. Run in well-lit areas or with a headlamp. 3. Run with a friend or tell someone where you're going.

Remember to always stay safe and adapt to the weather conditions.
