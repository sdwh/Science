716 - What is cuisine, and how does it vary around the world?

Cuisine is the style of cooking and the different types of food that people in a particular region or country eat.

Imagine going on a world tour, and at each stop, you try the local food. You'll notice it's different in each place. That's because cuisine varies around the world due to things like:

  1. Local ingredients: People use ingredients that grow in their area, like fish in Japan and spices in India.
  2. Culture and traditions: Each culture has its own special way of cooking and preparing food, like pasta in Italy or tacos in Mexico.
  3. Geography: People who live near the ocean might eat more seafood, while those in deserts might eat more meat and veggies.
  4. History: Food can be influenced by other cultures that have visited or trade with a place, like Chinese food in the US.

So, cuisine is like a big flavor puzzle, with each piece shaped by the people, place, and history of a region.
