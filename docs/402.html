How do phytoplankton contribute to ocean health?

Phytoplankton are tiny plants that live in the ocean. They help keep the ocean healthy in several ways:

  1. Produce Oxygen: Phytoplankton produce oxygen when they make food from sunlight, which helps all the animals in the ocean breathe.
  2. Food Source: They are a food source for many ocean animals, like fish, whales, and other tiny creatures.
  3. Clean the Water: Phytoplankton absorb bad substances like excess nutrients and pollutants from the water, which helps keep the ocean clean.

So, phytoplankton are like superheroes for the ocean, keeping it clean and healthy for all the creatures that live there.
