1540 - Why is there a need for water resource recycling and reuse?

We need to recycle and reuse water because:

  1. Water is limited: Only 2.5% of the Earth's water is freshwater, and most of it is frozen in glaciers or underground.
  2. Population growth: More people means more water needed for drinking, washing, and flushing.
  3. Water waste: A lot of water is wasted or used only once, like in factories, farms, or after washing our cars.
  4. Climate change: Changes in weather patterns can cause droughts, making water even scarcer.
  5. Environmental protection: Recycling and reusing water helps keep our rivers, lakes, and oceans clean by reducing the amount of wastewater released into them.

Recycling and reusing water helps save this precious resource for us, animals, and plants to use in the future.
