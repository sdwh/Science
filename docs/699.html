699 - What is the importance of traffic rules and regulations?

The importance of traffic rules and regulations is to keep people safe on the road. They help:

  1. Prevent accidents and injuries
  2. Avoid traffic jams and confusion
  3. Make sure everyone knows what to do on the road
  4. Protect people who walk, bike, or drive
  5. Keep our roads organized and smooth

By following traffic rules, we can make our roads safer and more enjoyable for everyone.
