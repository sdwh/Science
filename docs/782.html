782 - What should you do if you encounter wildlife while camping?

If you encounter wildlife while camping, follow these steps:

  1. Stay calm: Keep your voice low and movements slow to avoid scaring the animal.
  2. Keep distance: Back away slowly and give the animal space. Do not approach it.
  3. Protect food and trash: Keep food and trash in sealed containers to avoid attracting wildlife.
  4. Do not feed: Never feed wild animals, as this can make them aggressive.
  5. Make noise: If you're hiking, make loud noises to alert animals of your presence.
  6. Seek help: If the animal appears aggressive or threatened, seek help from a park ranger or an adult.

Remember, respect wild animals and their space for a safe and enjoyable camping experience.
