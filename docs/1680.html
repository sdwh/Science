1680 - What are the uses of lens since image formed by light passing through it can be analyzed by lens formula?

A lens is a very useful tool that helps us see things more clearly and even farther away. Here are some of its cool uses:

  1. Glasses and Contact Lenses: Lenses help people with poor eyesight see clearly by bending light to focus on their retina.
  2. Microscopes and Telescopes: Lenses magnify small objects or faraway places, like cells, stars, or planets, so we can study them better.
  3. Cameras: Lenses focus light onto a sensor or film, creating images we can see later.
  4. Magnifying Glasses: Lenses make things look bigger, like when you use a magnifying glass to read small text.
  5. Projectors and TVs: Lenses help create images on a screen, making it possible for us to watch movies or TV shows.
  6. Laser Technology: Lenses focus laser beams to make them stronger or change their path.
  7. Binoculars and Sunglasses: Lenses help us see distant objects clearly or protect our eyes from the sun.

The lens formula (1/f = 1/di + 1/do) helps us understand how lenses work and how we can use them to create the best images.
