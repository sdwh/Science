What are some environmentally friendly modes of transportation?

There are many environmentally friendly modes of transportation. Here are some:

  1. Bicycles: They don't pollute and are great exercise.
  2. Walking: Another way to stay active and reduce carbon footprint.
  3. Electric cars: They run on batteries, not gasoline, which reduces pollution.
  4. Hybrid cars: They use both gasoline and electricity, making them cleaner than regular cars.
  5. Trains: Often powered by electricity, trains can carry many people, reducing individual car pollution.
  6. Buses: Many buses are now electric or hybrid, making them a cleaner option for public transport.
  7. Scooters: Electric scooters are another alternative to traditional cars.
  8. Skateboards: They're fun, eco-friendly, and great exercise.
