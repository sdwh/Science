1156 - What sounds can be found in our surroundings?

Sounds in our surroundings can include:

  1. Birds chirping or singing
  2. Dogs barking or animals making noise
  3. Car horns or vehicles passing by
  4. Traffic noises like cars, buses, and trucks
  5. People talking, laughing, or shouting
  6. Music or the TV from a nearby house
  7. Ocean waves or water flowing if you live near a beach or river
  8. Leaves rustling in the wind or trees swaying
  9. Wind blowing, knocking objects over
  10. Alarms or sirens from emergency vehicles or buildings
