1517 - How is molar concentration represented in volume terms.

Molar concentration, or molarity, can be represented in volume terms as moles of a substance per liter of a solution. It's usually written as "moles per liter" or "M".

Example: 1M of a solution means there's 1 mole of the substance in 1 liter of that solution.
