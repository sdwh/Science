What is the importance of mangroves and salt marshes?

Mangroves and salt marshes are really important for our planet. Here's why:

  1. Home for animals: They provide a safe home for many animals like fish, birds, shrimp, and crabs. These animals rely on mangroves and salt marshes for food and shelter.
  2. Protect from storms: Mangroves and salt marshes help protect coastlines from big storms and strong waves. They act like a natural barrier, keeping people and homes safe.
  3. Clean the water: They help clean the water by filtering out bad stuff like pollutants and sediments. This makes the water safer for animals and humans.
  4. Prevent erosion: Mangroves and salt marshes help prevent erosion (when the soil gets washed away) by holding the soil in place.
  5. Help fight climate change: They absorb carbon dioxide, a bad gas that contributes to climate change.

So, mangroves and salt marshes are really important for keeping our planet healthy and safe!
