How can we create a safe and clean environment at home?

To create a safe and clean environment at home, follow these simple steps:

  1. Clean regularly: Sweep, mop, and dust regularly to keep your home free from dirt and dust.
  2. Throw away trash: Don't leave trash lying around - recycle or throw it away in the bin.
  3. Wash your hands: Wash your hands with soap and water before eating and after using the bathroom.
  4. Organize clutter: Keep toys, books, and other things in their proper place to avoid tripping hazards.
  5. Use cleaning products: Use gentle cleaning products to keep your home germ-free.
  6. Keep electrical cords safe: Avoid tripping over cords and keep them away from water.
  7. Take care of pets: Clean up after your pets to keep your home clean and germ-free.
  8. Check for hazards: Check your home for hazards like broken toys or loose rugs that could cause accidents.

Remember, a clean and safe home is a happy home!
