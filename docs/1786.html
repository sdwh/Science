1786 - What is advanced material?

An advanced material is a special kind of material that has extraordinary properties or abilities that make it stronger, lighter, or more useful than regular materials.

Examples include:

  • Kevlar (used in bulletproof vests)
  • Carbon fiber (used in sports cars and bicycles)
  • Graphene (a super-strong, super-thin material)
  • Memory foam (used in comfy shoes and mattresses)
  • Smart glass (changes from clear to dark with electricity)

These materials are created using special technologies to make them better suited for various applications.
