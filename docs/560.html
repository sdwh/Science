How can we promote healthy relationships with friends and family?

To promote healthy relationships with friends and family:

  1. Communicate openly: Share your feelings and thoughts with them, and listen to theirs.
  2. Respect each other: Treat others the way you want to be treated.
  3. Be supportive: Encourage and help each other when needed.
  4. Spend quality time together: Do fun activities or just hang out.
  5. Apologize when needed: Say 'sorry' if you've made a mistake.
  6. Set boundaries: Know what's okay and what's not okay in a relationship.
  7. Be honest and trustworthy: Keep your promises and don't lie.

Remember, healthy relationships make us happy and strong.
