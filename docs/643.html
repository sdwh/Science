643 - What are the main uses of petroleum in our daily lives?

Petroleum is super useful, and we use it in many things. Here are some of the main uses:

  1. Fuel for cars and trucks: Gasoline and diesel are made from petroleum, so it helps us move around.
  2. Heating homes and buildings: Petroleum is used to make heating oil that keeps us warm.
  3. Making plastics: Many plastics, like water bottles and toys, are made from petroleum.
  4. Manufacturing lots of products: Petroleum is used to make things like clothes, cosmetics, and even medicines.
  5. Making lubricants: Petroleum helps keep machines running smoothly by making lubricants like motor oil.
  6. Aviation fuel: Planes run on a special type of petroleum fuel called jet fuel.
  7. Creating many household items: Petroleum is used in many everyday items, like soaps, detergents, and even some types of food packaging.

We use petroleum in so many ways, it's hard to imagine life without it.
