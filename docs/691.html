691 - What are the different types of transportation?

There are many types of transportation. Here are some:

  1. Land Transportation:
  2. Cars
  3. Buses
  4. Trucks
  5. Trains
  6. Bicycles

  7. Motorcycles

  8. Air Transportation:

  9. Airplanes

  10. Helicopters

  11. Water Transportation:

  12. Boats
  13. Ships

  14. Ferries

  15. Space Transportation:

  16. Rockets (for astronauts and satellites)

Remember, people use different types of transportation depending on where they're going and how fast they want to get there.
