318 - Why is it important to recycle materials?

Recycling is important because it helps our planet in three main ways:

  1. Conserves natural resources: Recycling helps to reuse materials that we would otherwise take from the Earth, like wood, water, and minerals.
  2. Saves energy: Making new things from recycled materials uses less energy than making things from scratch.
  3. Reduces trash: Recycling helps to decrease the amount of waste that ends up in landfills and oceans, which keeps our environment clean and safe.

So, by recycling, we're helping to protect our planet and keep it healthy for future generations.
