1791 - What is an effective method for resource conservation?

One effective method for resource conservation is the "3Rs":

  1. Reduce: Use less of things you need, like turning off the lights when not in use or using fewer paper towels.
  2. Reuse: Find a new use for old things, like using a jar for storing pencils or a plastic bag for carrying lunch.
  3. Recycle: Turn old things into new ones, like recycling cans and bottles into new paper and other materials.

Remembering the 3Rs can help you make a big difference in saving our planet's resources.
