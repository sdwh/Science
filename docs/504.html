504 - How do zoos ensure the safety of both animals and visitors?

Zoos ensure the safety of both animals and visitors by:

  1. Barriers and enclosures: They build strong walls, fences, and moats to keep animals inside and visitors out.
  2. Trained staff: Zookeepers and security guards are trained to handle animals and emergencies.
  3. Rules and signs: Zoos have rules and signs to remind visitors not to touch, feed, or tease the animals.
  4. Safety nets and cameras: some zoos have safety nets and cameras to monitor the animals and visitors.
  5. Animal care: Zoos provide good care to the animals, including food, water, and medical checks, to keep them healthy and calm.
