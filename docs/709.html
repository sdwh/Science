709 - What are some safety measures we should follow while traveling?

When traveling, it's essential to stay safe. Here are some key safety measures to follow:

  1. Stay with an adult: If you're a kid, always stay with a parent or guardian, especially in crowded areas.
  2. Carry ID and contact info: Carry a card with your name, phone number, and address in case you get lost.
  3. Be aware of your surroundings: Keep an eye on your belongings and be mindful of what's happening around you.
  4. Use reputable transportation: Only use licensed taxis or ride-sharing services.
  5. Keep valuables secure: Keep your money, phone, and other valuables safe and secure.
  6. Stay hydrated and energized: Drink plenty of water and eat regular meals to avoid getting tired or weak.
  7. Follow local rules and customs: Respect local laws and traditions to avoid trouble.
  8. Stay connected: Keep your phone charged and make sure someone knows your travel plans.

Remember, safety is everyone's responsibility, so always be aware and take care of yourself while traveling!
