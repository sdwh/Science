814 - What role do hobbies play in reducing stress and improving mental health?

Imagine your mind is like a computer with too many tabs open. Hobbies help close some of those tabs and clear your mind. They play a big role in reducing stress and improving mental health in three ways:

  1. Distracts from worries: When you're doing something you love, you focus on that instead of your worries. It's like taking a break from your problems.
  2. Releases happy feelings: Many hobbies make you feel good or excited, which is like adding happy notes to your mind diary. This helps balance out the stressful thoughts.
  3. Gives a sense of control: When you're good at a hobby, it makes you feel confident and in control. This feeling can carry over to other areas of your life, helping you feel more capable of handling stress.

So, hobbies are like a reset button for your mind. They help you relax, feel good, and gain confidence.
