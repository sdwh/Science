What are some tips for cooking on a budget?

Here are some tips for cooking on a budget:

  1. Plan your meals: Think about what you want to cook before you go shopping, and make a list. This way, you won't buy too much food that might go to waste.
  2. Shop for staples: Buy basic ingredients like rice, pasta, and beans, which are cheap and can be used in many meals.
  3. Use cheaper protein: Instead of buying expensive meat, try using beans, lentils, or eggs as your main protein source.
  4. Shop at discount stores: Look for sales and discounts at your local grocery store, and consider shopping at discount stores or farmer's markets.
  5. Make leftovers work: Use leftover food to make new meals, so you don't waste anything.
  6. Avoid pre-packaged food: Pre-packaged food is often more expensive than ingredients you can buy in bulk.
  7. Cook in bulk: Make big batches of food, like soups or stews, and freeze them for later.
