What role does automation play in modern manufacturing?

In modern manufacturing, automation plays a big role. It means using machines and computers to do many tasks instead of people. Automation helps in several ways:

  1. Faster production: Machines can work 24/7 without getting tired, making production faster and more efficient.
  2. Improved quality: Machines can be more precise and make fewer mistakes than humans, leading to higher quality products.
  3. Reduced costs: Automation can save money by reducing labor costs and minimizing waste.
  4. Increased safety: Machines can handle hazardous tasks, keeping people safe from accidents.
  5. Customization: Automation allows for easy changes to production, making it possible to create customized products quickly and efficiently.

Examples of automation in manufacturing include robotic assembly lines, 3D printing, and computer-controlled machines. This helps make many everyday products, like cars, electronics, and clothing, more efficiently and effectively.
