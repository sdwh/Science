424 - How do pulleys lift heavy objects?

Imagine you're trying to lift a heavy box. If you use just your hands, you have to lift the whole weight by yourself. That can be really hard.

A pulley is a simple machine that helps make lifting easier. It's like a wheel with a rope that goes around it. When you pull the rope, it wraps around the wheel, making the weight lift up.

Here's how it helps:

  1. The rope spreads the weight: The rope wraps around the pulley, so the weight gets shared between two sides of the rope.
  2. Less force needed: Because the weight is shared, you only need to pull half as hard to lift the weight up.
  3. Easier lifting: By using a pulley, you can lift heavy objects with less effort. It's like getting help from a friend, but without needing a second person!

So, pulleys make lifting heavy objects easier by spreading the weight and sharing the force.
