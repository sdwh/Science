605 - What role do insects play in the ecosystem?

Insects play a vital role in the ecosystem. Here are a few ways they help:

  1. Food source: Insects are food for many animals like birds, bats, and other insects.
  2. Pollination: Bees, butterflies, and other insects help plants make seeds by transferring pollen from one flower to another.
  3. Decomposition: Insects like beetles and ants help break down dead plants and animals, turning them into nutrient-rich soil.
  4. Soil aeration: Some insects, like ants and ground beetles, dig tunnels that help air and water reach plant roots.
  5. Pest control: Some insects, like ladybugs and lacewings, eat other insects that can harm plants.

Insects are an important part of the ecosystem, and without them, many plants and animals would struggle to survive.
