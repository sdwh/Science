1392 - What are the 5Rs of resource usage?

The 5Rs of resource usage are a simple way to help us use the Earth's resources wisely. They are:

  1. Reduce: Use less of something, like turning off the tap while brushing your teeth to save water.
  2. Reuse: Use something again instead of throwing it away, like using a reusable water bottle or bag.
  3. Recycle: Turn old things into new things, like turning plastic bottles into new toys.
  4. Rot: Let food waste break down naturally, like composting fruit and veggie scraps.
  5. Refuse: Say no to things you don't need, like refusing a plastic straw or plastic bag.

By following these 5Rs, we can help take care of our planet!
