724 - What are some popular dishes from different countries?

Here are some popular dishes from different countries:

  1. China: Dumplings (steamed or fried, filled with meat or veggies)
  2. Italy: Pizza (with tomato sauce and cheese) and Pasta (with many sauces)
  3. India: Chicken Tikka Masala (a creamy, spicy chicken dish) and Naan Bread (leavened flatbread)
  4. Japan: Sushi (vinegared rice and raw fish rolls) and Ramen (a noodle soup)
  5. Mexico: Tacos (corn tortillas filled with meat, veggies, and salsa) and Quesadillas (cheesy folded tortillas)
  6. Thailand: Pad Thai (stir-fried noodles with veggies and peanuts) and Tom Yum Soup (spicy and sour soup)
  7. Brazil: Feijoada (a bean stew with beef and pork) and Churrasco (grilled meat, usually steak)
  8. Greece: Gyro (a meat-filled wrap with tomato, onion, and tzatziki sauce)
  9. South Korea: Bibimbap (mixed rice bowl with veggies and a fried egg) and Kimchi (spicy fermented veggies)
  10. Spain: Paella (a rice dish with seafood and saffron) and Tapas (small plates of food, like tortilla española)

These are just a few examples, and there are many more delicious dishes to explore from around the world.
