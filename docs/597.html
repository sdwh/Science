What is recycling, and why is it important?

Recycling is the process of turning old or used things into new things. This helps to protect the Earth by:

  1. Saving natural resources (like water and energy)
  2. Reducing pollution and garbage
  3. Conserving space in landfills

Think of it like this: When you recycle a plastic bottle, it can become a new toy or another bottle, instead of being thrown away and ending up in a big pile of trash.

By recycling, you're helping to keep our planet clean and make sure we have enough resources for the future. It's like doing a small job to help the Earth, and it makes a big difference when we all do it together.
