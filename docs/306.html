306 - How does the water cycle affect our environment?

The water cycle is like a big circle where water moves from the Earth to the air and back to the Earth. It affects our environment in several ways:

  1. Gives us rain and snow: The water cycle brings us the water we need to drink and to grow plants.
  2. Makes weather: It helps create weather patterns like rain, snow, and even hurricanes.
  3. Fills lakes and rivers: The water cycle refills our lakes, rivers, and oceans, which supports all sorts of plants and animals.
  4. Keeps the Earth cool: The water cycle helps regulate the Earth's temperature by moving heat around the globe.
  5. Replenishes groundwater: It helps fill up underground water supplies that we use for drinking, irrigation, and other things.

Overall, the water cycle is very important for keeping our planet healthy and supporting all living things.
