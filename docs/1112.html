Science Common Sense

1112 - What kinds of creatures can survive in different environments

There are lots of creatures that can survive in different environments. Here are some examples:

  1. Deserts:
  2. Camels (they store water in their humps)
  3. Snakes (they can go without water for a long time)

  4. Scorpions (they hide underground to avoid heat)

  5. Oceans:

  6. Dolphins (they can hold their breath underwater)
  7. Octopuses (they have special skin that helps them breathe underwater)

  8. Jellyfish (they can float in the water and catch food with their tentacles)

  9. Polar regions:

  10. Penguins (they have a thick layer of feathers to keep warm)
  11. Polar bears (they have white fur and a layer of fat to stay warm)

  12. Seals (they have a thick layer of fat to stay warm in the cold water)

  13. Mountains:

  14. Mountain goats (they have strong legs to climb on rocks)
  15. Yaks (they have thick fur to stay warm)

  16. Eagles (they can fly high and spot food from afar)

  17. Rainforests:

  18. Monkeys (they swing from trees and eat fruits)
  19. Sloths (they hide in trees and eat leaves)
  20. Frogs (they jump from one plant to another and eat insects)

These are just a few examples of creatures that can survive in different environments. Each of them has special features that help them adapt to their surroundings.
