161 - How do enzymes affect chemical reactions in our bodies?

Our bodies have tiny helpers called enzymes that make chemical reactions happen faster and easier. They're like super-speedy chefs who mix and cook food (like proteins and carbs) so our bodies can use them for energy.

Imagine you're trying to light a fire, but it's taking a long time because the wood is damp. An enzyme is like a match that helps spark the fire quickly and easily. In our bodies, enzymes help spark chemical reactions so they happen faster and more smoothly. Without enzymes, our bodies would get stuck and couldn't work properly.
