830 - What are some famous artists, and what styles did they use?

Here are some famous artists and their styles:

  1. Leonardo da Vinci: Renaissance (mix of realism and detailed drawings). He's famous for the 'Mona Lisa'.
  2. Vincent van Gogh: Impressionism (bold colors and thick brushstrokes). His paintings like 'Sunflowers' are iconic.
  3. Pablo Picasso: Cubism (breaking objects into shapes and reassembling them). He's known for 'Guernica'.
  4. Claude Monet: Impressionism (capturing light and colors). His paintings like 'Water Lilies' are famous.
  5. Michelangelo: Renaissance (sculpting and painting with realistic detail). His sculptures like the 'David' statue are renowned.
  6. Salvador Dali: Surrealism (dreamlike and strange paintings). His paintings like 'The Persistence of Memory' are popular.
  7. Johannes Vermeer: Realism (detailed and realistic paintings of everyday life). His paintings like 'Girl with a Pearl Earring' are famous.
  8. Andy Warhol: Pop Art (using popular culture in art). His paintings like 'Campbell's Soup Cans' are iconic.

These are just a few examples, but there are many more amazing artists out there!
