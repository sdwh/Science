How do plants benefit indoor environments?

Plants are super helpful indoors. They:

  1. Clean the air: Plants absorb bad stuff from the air, like pollution, and release oxygen.
  2. Help you relax: Being around plants can calm you down and make you feel happy.
  3. Keep it cozy: Plants help keep the air fresh and regulate the temperature in a room.
  4. Reduce noise: Some plants can even help block out loud noises and make your space quieter.
  5. Make it look nice: Plants add some green color to your room and make it look more beautiful.

Overall, having plants indoors is good for your health, happiness, and your space.
