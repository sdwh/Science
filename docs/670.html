670 - How do we purify water for consumption?

There are several ways to purify water for consumption. Here are the most common methods:

  1. Boiling: Boil the water for at least 1 minute to kill all the bad bacteria and germs.
  2. Filtration: Use a filter like a water filter pitcher or a faucet-mounted filter to remove dirt, dust, and other particles.
  3. Water Distillation: Boil the water and then collect the steam, which is purified water.
  4. Chlorination: Add chlorine or bleach to the water to kill bacteria and germs.
  5. Reverse Osmosis: Use a special membrane to remove impurities from the water by pushing it through tiny holes.
  6. Ultraviolet (UV) Light: Expose the water to UV light to kill bacteria and germs.

These methods help to make the water safe to drink and remove any yucky stuff that might be in it.
