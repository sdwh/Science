1016 - What is sustainable development?

Sustainable development means taking care of the Earth so that our planet stays healthy for us and for future generations. It's like being responsible for a garden.

Imagine you have a beautiful garden where you grow fruits and vegetables. To keep it healthy, you have to use water wisely, not pollute the soil, and take care of its surroundings. That way, your garden will continue to grow and provide you with food.

In the same way, sustainable development is about using the Earth's resources (like water, air, and land) wisely, reducing pollution, and protecting nature. This way, our planet stays healthy and happy for everyone.
