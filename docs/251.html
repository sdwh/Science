251 - What are tectonic plates?

Tectonic plates are huge puzzle pieces that make up the Earth's surface. They are big slabs of rock that move slowly on the Earth's surface, and sometimes bump or rub against each other. These plates are in constant motion, creating mountains, volcanoes, and sometimes earthquakes when they move or collide with each other.

Think of it like a jigsaw puzzle with huge pieces that fit together, but they're always moving and changing shape.
