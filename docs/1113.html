1113 - What are the characteristics of different environments on the Earth's surface such as rocks, sand, and soil?

Let's explore the characteristics of different environments on the Earth's surface.

  1. Rocks:
  2. They're hard and don't break easily.
  3. Can be big or small, like boulders or pebbles.

  4. Not very good at holding water.

  5. Sand:

  6. It's soft and can be easily shifted by wind or water.
  7. Mainly consists of small rock particles.

  8. Also not very good at holding water.

  9. Soil:

  10. It's made of a mix of small rock particles, air, water, and living things.
  11. Good at holding water and nutrients for plants to grow.
  12. Often has different layers or types, like clay, silt, or loam.

These environments can change due to natural processes like weathering, erosion, or human activities.
