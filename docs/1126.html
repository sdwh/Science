1126 - What forms of water exist on Earth?

There are three main forms of water on Earth:

  1. Solid: This is ice. You can find it in glaciers, ice cubes, or even the ice you make in your freezer.
  2. Liquid: This is the water you drink, play with, or swim in. You can find it in oceans, rivers, lakes, or even the water you use to wash your hands.
  3. Gas: This is called water vapor. You can see it as steam when you take a hot shower or boil water. It's also in the air we breathe, and it helps create clouds that can bring us rain.

These three forms of water help make our planet a wonderful and interesting place to live.
