How do scientists and engineers work together to improve electricity technology?

Scientists and engineers work together to improve electricity technology by following these steps:

  1. Scientists research: They study the basics of electricity, like how it flows and how to generate it.
  2. Engineers apply: They use the scientist's discoveries to design and build new technologies, like better power plants or more efficient power grids.
  3. Testing and redesign: Engineers test their designs, and if they don't work as expected, scientists help figure out what went wrong and how to fix it.
  4. Collaboration and innovation: Scientists and engineers keep working together to come up with new and better ideas, like solar panels or wind turbines, to improve electricity technology.

By working together, scientists and engineers can create new and better ways to generate, transmit, and use electricity, making our lives more convenient and sustainable.
