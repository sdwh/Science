1023 - What is the difference between a herbivore, a carnivore, and an omnivore?

Imagine you're an animal and you need food to survive.

  • A herbivore is an animal that only eats plants, fruits, and leaves. Examples: cow, deer, and a rabbit.
  • A carnivore is an animal that only eats meat, like other animals. Examples: lions, tigers, and sharks.
  • An omnivore is an animal that eats both plants and other animals. Examples: humans, bears, and pigs.
