What is robotics?

Robotics is a field of science and engineering that deals with designing, building, and using robots. A robot is a machine that can be programmed to do things automatically, like a computer, and can also move around or perform physical tasks, like a machine.

Think of a robot like a super-smart machine that can help us do things like clean our homes, help doctors in hospitals, or even explore space. Robotics is all about using technology to make our lives easier and more fun!
