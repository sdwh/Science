319 - How does the Earth’s atmosphere protect us?

The Earth's atmosphere is like a big shield that protects us from harm. Here are a few ways it helps:

  1. Blocks bad sun rays: The atmosphere blocks some of the sun's harmful rays, like UV rays, that can hurt our skin and eyes.
  2. Keeps us warm: The atmosphere traps some of the sun's heat, keeping us warm at night and comfortable during the day.
  3. Protects from space rocks: The atmosphere burns up small rocks and space debris that might fall from space, keeping us safe from harm.
  4. Maintains air pressure: The atmosphere's air pressure keeps our bodies from swelling up or getting hurt from the lack of pressure.

Overall, the Earth's atmosphere is like a safety blanket that keeps us safe and healthy.
