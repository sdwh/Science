968 - What is the role of roots in a plant's growth and stability?

Roots play a very important role in a plant's growth and stability. Here are some of their main jobs:

  1. Support: They help hold the plant upright and prevent it from falling over.
  2. Water and food: Roots absorb water and nutrients from the soil, which helps the plant grow and stay healthy.
  3. Anchoring: They keep the plant stuck in the ground, so it doesn't get washed away by wind or water.
  4. Storage: Some roots, like carrots and potatoes, store food for the plant to use later.

Overall, roots are like the underground legs of a plant, helping it grow strong and steady.
