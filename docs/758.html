What are some activities that can improve cardiovascular fitness?

There are many fun activities that can improve your cardiovascular fitness. Here are some:

  1. Running or jogging
  2. Swimming
  3. Cycling (biking)
  4. Dancing (like Zumba)
  5. Jumping rope
  6. Playing sports like soccer, basketball, or tennis
  7. Walking quickly (brisk walking)
  8. Hiking
  9. Playing tag with friends
  10. Skateboarding or rollerblading

Remember, all you need is 30-60 minutes, 3-4 times a week, to stay heart-healthy and fit. Always ask a grown-up for permission and supervision when starting new exercises.
