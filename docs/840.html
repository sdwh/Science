840 - What is the difference between the left and right hemispheres of the brain?

Imagine your brain is divided into two teams that work together.

  • The left hemisphere is like a planner and organizer. It helps with:
  • Logic (solving math problems and puzzles)
  • Language (speaking, reading, and writing)
  • Remembering facts and details

  • Following rules and patterns

  • The right hemisphere is like a creative artist. It helps with:

  • Imagination and creativity (art, music, imagination)
  • Recognizing faces and understanding emotions
  • Visualizing and understanding big pictures (like maps or stories)
  • Intuition and making quick decisions

Both teams work together to help you think, learn, and solve problems.
