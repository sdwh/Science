966 - How can individuals stay safe during a disaster like a tornado or earthquake?

During a disaster like a tornado or earthquake, stay safe by following these steps:

  1. Tornado:
  2. Go to a basement or storm cellar.
  3. If not available, hide in an interior room or hallway on the lowest floor, away from windows and doors.

  4. Get under a sturdy table or desk and cover your head.

  5. Earthquake:

  6. 'Drop, Cover, and Hold On': immediately drop to the ground, take cover under a sturdy piece of furniture, and hold onto it to protect yourself from falling objects.

General tips: - Stay away from windows, doors, and exterior walls. - Keep important phone numbers handy, like emergency contacts. - Always follow evacuation instructions from authorities. - Practice disaster drills at home and school so you're prepared.

Remember, being prepared and knowing what to do can save your life during a disaster.
