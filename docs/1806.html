1806 - What are the main building materials derived from?

Main building materials are derived from these main sources:

  1. Plants: Wood (trees), bamboo, and thatched roofs come from plants.
  2. Rocks and Minerals: Stones, bricks, concrete, cement, and glass are made from rocks and minerals.
  3. Animals: Leather, wool, and even animal bones (used in some special cement) come from animals.
  4. Earth: Mud, clay, and soil are used to make adobe houses and bricks.
  5. Recycled Materials: Some buildings use recycled materials like recycled glass, plastic, and metal.

These are the main sources of building materials.
