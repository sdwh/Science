776 - What are the essential items to pack for a camping trip?

A camping trip can be so much fun. To make sure you're safe and have a great time, here are the essential items to pack:

  1. Shelter and sleeping gear:
  2. Tent
  3. Sleeping bag

  4. Sleeping pad

  5. Clothing:

  6. Extra clothes (including a rain jacket)
  7. Warm layers for cold nights

  8. Sturdy shoes or hiking boots

  9. Food and cooking gear:

  10. Food for meals and snacks
  11. Water bottles or a water filter
  12. Camping stove or a portable grill

  13. Cooking utensils and plates

  14. Lighting and safety:

  15. Flashlights or headlamps
  16. Extra batteries
  17. First aid kit

  18. Fire starters (matches, kindling, etc.)

  19. Personal items:

  20. Toiletries (toothbrush, toothpaste, etc.)
  21. Sunscreen and insect repellent
  22. Personal medications
