535 - What is the significance of keeping a balanced ecosystem?

Keeping a balanced ecosystem is important because it ensures that our planet stays healthy. Imagine an ecosystem as a big team:

  • Plants clean the air we breathe,
  • Animals help spread seeds for more plants to grow, and
  • Rivers and lakes provide water for plants and animals.

If one team member goes missing, it can affect the entire team (ecosystem). A balanced ecosystem:

  • Helps keep the air and water clean
  • Gives homes to plants and animals
  • Makes sure we have food to eat

So, keeping the ecosystem balanced is crucial for the well-being of our planet and everyone living on it.
