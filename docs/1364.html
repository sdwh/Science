1364 - What can all citizens do to conserve biodiversity?

To conserve biodiversity, all citizens can:

  1. Plant trees and flowers: This helps create homes for animals and insects.
  2. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Don't waste water and reduce trash to keep our environment clean.
  3. Use eco-friendly products: Choose products that don't harm animals or the environment.
  4. Save water: Use less water to help animals and plants that need it.
  5. Don't litter: Keep our oceans and parks clean by throwing trash away properly.
  6. Support conservation efforts: Learn about and help organizations that protect animals and their homes.
  7. Respect wildlife: Don't disturb or touch animals in the wild, and keep a safe distance.

Remember, every small action helps to conserve biodiversity!
