461 - How do we maintain household appliances for longevity?

To make your household appliances last longer, follow these simple tips:

  1. Read the manual: Understand how to use and care for each appliance.
  2. Clean regularly: Dust and dirt can damage appliances, so wipe them down often.
  3. Check filters: Clean or replace filters in appliances like air conditioners, vacuum cleaners, and refrigerators.
  4. Don't overload: Avoid overloading appliances like washing machines and refrigerators, as it can cause them to break down.
  5. Check expiration dates: Replace expired or worn-out parts, like water filters, to ensure they keep working properly.
  6. Unplug when not in use: Unplug appliances to prevent overheating and save energy.
  7. Run maintenance cycles: Run maintenance cycles on appliances like dishwashers and washing machines to keep them clean and running smoothly.

By following these tips, you can help extend the life of your household appliances and save money on repairs.
