413 - What is the significance of biodiversity in marine environments?

Biodiversity in marine environments is very important. Here's why:

  1. Healthy Oceans: Many different species help keep the oceans clean and healthy.
  2. Food for Humans: Lots of marine animals, like fish, provide food for us.
  3. Medicine: Marine animals and plants can give us new medicines to fight diseases.
  4. Climate Balance: Marine life helps keep the air clean and stable by absorbing carbon dioxide.
  5. Shore Protection: Coral reefs and mangroves protect coastlines from big storms and waves.

Having many different species in marine environments helps keep everything balanced and working properly.
