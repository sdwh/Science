How can I display or share my artwork with others?

There are many ways to share your artwork with others. Here are some ideas:

  1. School art shows: Ask your teacher if your school has an art show or exhibit. You can display your artwork there.
  2. Social media: Share photos of your artwork on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, or TikTok. Use hashtags to reach more people.
  3. Online art platforms: Websites like DeviantArt, ArtStation, or Behance allow you to create a portfolio and share your artwork with others.
  4. Local art galleries: Some local art galleries have exhibits for young artists or students. You can ask if they'd be interested in showing your work.
  5. Art competitions: Enter online art competitions or contests in your community to showcase your work.
  6. Print and display at home: Print out your artwork and display it at home or in your room. You can also give prints to friends and family.
  7. Start a blog: Create a blog to share your artwork, describe your creative process, and connect with other artists.

Remember, sharing your artwork can be a great way to get feedback, inspire others, and build your confidence as an artist.
