What safety precautions should you take while fishing?

When fishing, follow these safety precautions:

  1. Wear a life jacket: Always wear a life jacket near or on water to stay safe in case you fall in.
  2. Check the weather: Avoid fishing during bad weather like thunderstorms or strong winds.
  3. Watch where you step: Be careful not to slip and fall on rocks or near water.
  4. Bring a buddy: Fish with a friend or family member so you can help each other if needed.
  5. Follow local rules: Learn and respect local fishing rules, like protected areas and catch limits.
  6. Be careful with hooks and lines: Handle fish hooks and lines carefully, and keep them away from your face and eyes.
  7. Bring sun protection: Wear sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun.
  8. Respect the water: Don't pollute the water or disturb fish habitats.

Remember to always be prepared and have fun!
