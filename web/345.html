What is ecology, and why is it important?

Ecology is the study of how all living things interact with each other and their environment. This includes animals, plants, and even tiny germs. It's like looking at the world as a big web where everything is connected.

Ecology is important because it helps us understand how to keep the Earth healthy and balanced. This includes:

  • Keeping the air and water clean
  • Protecting animals and their homes
  • Planting trees and gardens to help clean the air and provide food
  • Learning how to use natural resources without harming the environment

By taking care of the Earth, we're also taking care of ourselves and our future.
