What is the role of technology in disaster prediction and management?

Technology helps predict and manage disasters in several ways:

  1. Predicting disasters: Satellites in space and sensors on the ground can track weather patterns, earthquakes, and floods. Computers analyze the data to predict when and where a disaster might strike.
  2. Warning systems: Automated systems can quickly send warnings to people in the affected area through mobile phones, sirens, or social media.
  3. Monitoring situations: Drones and cameras can capture images of the disaster area, helping rescue teams plan their response.
  4. Coordinating responses: Technology helps emergency teams communicate and work together, making response efforts more efficient.
  5. Analyzing damage: Technology, like drones with cameras, helps assess the damage after a disaster, so aid can be targeted where it's most needed.

Overall, technology saves lives and helps reduce the impact of disasters.
