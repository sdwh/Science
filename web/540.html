How do our choices impact the planet?

Our choices have a big impact on the planet. Here are some examples:

  1. Waste: When we use plastic bags, straws, and bottles, they can end up in oceans and hurt animals.
  2. Energy: Using too much energy at home and in cars can make the air dirty and contribute to climate change.
  3. Food: The food we choose to eat can help or hurt the planet. For example, choosing plant-based foods can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
  4. Water: Wasting water can harm animals and plants that need it to survive.
  5. Transportation: How we get around, like walking, biking, or driving, affects the air we breathe.

By making small changes to our daily choices, like reducing, reusing, and recycling, we can help protect the planet and make it a cleaner and healthier place for all living things.
