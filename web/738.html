What are some popular desserts, and how are they made?

Let's explore some popular desserts and their recipes:

  1. Chocolate Chip Cookies: Mix 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp salt, 1 cup butter, 3/4 cup sugar, 1 egg, and 1 cup chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet and bake at 375°F for 10 minutes.

  2. Brownies: Mix 1/2 cup melted butter, 2 cups sugar, 4 eggs, 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup cocoa powder, and 1 tsp vanilla. Bake in an 8-inch square baking dish at 350°F for 25-30 minutes.

  3. Apple Pie: Mix 2 cups flour, 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 cup butter (cold and cut into small pieces), and 1/4 cup ice water. Roll out the dough to fit a pie dish, fill with sliced apples, sugar, and spices, and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.

  4. Cheesecake: Mix 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup melted butter. Press the mixture into a springform pan. Mix 16 oz cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla. Pour the cream cheese mixture into the pan and bake at 350°F for 50-60 minutes.

  5. Ice Cream: Mix 2 cups cream, 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup sugar, and flavorings like vanilla. Freeze the mixture in an ice cream maker or a metal bowl and chill in the freezer until set.

  6. Meringues: Mix 1 cup sugar, 3 egg whites, and 1/2 tsp salt. Beat until fluffy and pipe onto a baking sheet. Bake in a low oven (200°F) for 2 hours until crispy.

Remember to always ask an adult for help while baking.
